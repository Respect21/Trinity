ABOUT US

Trinity Pool Global Limited is a leading provider of specialized services in swimming pool construction, water treatment, and purification.

We transform spaces with our expertise in creating visually stunning pools, elegant fountains, and captivating waterfalls. Our commitment to quality and innovation ensures that every project we undertake meets the highest standards. Beyond construction, we also offer a wide range of equipment and chemicals for pool maintenance, water treatment, and borehole management, catering to both residential and commercial needs.

Established in 2019 with the goal of redefining water-related solutions, Trinity Pool Global Limited began as a small enterprise focusing on bespoke swimming pool construction. Over time, our dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction has enabled us to grow into a well-respected name in the industry. We have successfully expanded our services to include comprehensive water treatment solutions and the supply of specialized chemicals, establishing long-term relationships with a diverse client base.
